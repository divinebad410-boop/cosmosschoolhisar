Who We Are

A School Built for Every Child's Potential

Cosmos Public School is an English-medium, co-educational institution offering residential, day boarding and day scholar options from Nursery to Class XII. Affiliated to CBSE, we offer Medical, Non-Medical and Arts streams for senior secondary classes.

  • Village Gangwa campus, 1.3 km from NH-65 — serene and focused learning environment
  • Atal Tinkering Lab (ATL) for innovation, coding and STEM education
  • Smart boards and digital classrooms with modern teaching methods
  • Strong emphasis on moral education, discipline and character development
